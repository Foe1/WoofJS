When to switch your student to WoofJS?

1.
They think they're done with Scratch

Your student may be bored or feel that they have outgrown coding in Scratch.
WoofJS may be particularly challenging for this type of student, but it can provide them with a more complex coding environment.

2.
They are actually done with Scratch

If your student is spending their time coding fighting against Scratch's limitations, it's time to switch to WoofJS.
With WoofJS your student can build multi-player games, complex data structures, and mobile-optimized games.
