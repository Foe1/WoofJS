What do students learn?

When to switch your student to WoofJS?

Scratch feels too "childish"

Your student may be older or feel that they have outgrown Scratch. WoofJS may be particularly challenging for this type of student, but it can provide them with a more complex coding environment.

They want to create more complex projects

If your student is fighting against Scratch's limitations, it's time to switch to WoofJS. With WoofJS your student can build multi-player games, complex data structures, and mobile games.

How much coding should my student know?

Before coding in WoofJS, we recommend students are very comfortable in Scratch. If they can create a game similar to this on their own, they are defintely ready for WoofJS!

After WoofJs, the possiblilites are endless. WoofJS allows students to feel confident coding in text-based languages. This means they will pick up any language they learn next much faster.
