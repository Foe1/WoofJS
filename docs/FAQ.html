Frequently Asked Questions

  • How old do my students need to be to use WoofJS?

     We recommend WoofJS for students after they have a basic understanding of Scratch. Typically, our youngest WoofJS students are around 10 years old.

  • How can I keep track of my students work?

     Your student can create an account and save their work as they go. This allows you to login at the beginning of class and get coding.

  • How much does it cost?

     WoofJS is completely free! We don't charge a membership fee and you can create, save, and code as many projects as you would like.

  • How do I teach WoofJS

     You can start you student by clicking on Get Started. We recommend they begin on the typealong tutorial and then delve into our other projects.

  • Can I teach this at my school?

     Yes! We would love to have you integrate WoofJS into your classroom. If you want help getting started, you can schedule a time to meet with our team here and we will go over evertyhing you need to know.

  • Do I need to Download Anything?

     Nope! You can code with WoofJS in any browser. WoofJS is completely free forever.
